Apex Martial Arts and Yoga Tribe

Peak Taekwondo and Apex Martial Arts and Yoga Tribe maintain an ongoing relationship that includes shared informational materials, online classes, and even testing participation. Many of the documents linked from our For students page originated with (and in some cases are still maintained by) Master Allen Shearer at Apex.

For more inforation on schedules, classes, and materials from Apex Martial Arts and Yoga Tribe, please visit their website.
