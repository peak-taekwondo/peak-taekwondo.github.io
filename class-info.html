Class information

Classes are taught through the City of Longmont as part of their official activities classes. We offer hour-and-a-half classes on Mondays and Wednesdays as well as an hour long class on Thursdays. All classes are held at the Izaak Walton Clubhouse on Sunset in Longmont.

Cost

Peak Taekwondo proudly offers students the benefit of a no contract payment plan. Instead of asking our students to sign up for a long-term contract, students register on a month-by-month basis through the City of Longmont. Additionally, our belt testing costs are very low, especially for colored belt tests, covering simply materials and external registration fees as applicable. The class registration cost for students is $100 per student for city residents, or $125 per student for non-residents. All payments should be made through the City of Longmont. See the Register for classes page for more information on how to register for classes.

Regular weekly class schedule

  • Mondays, from 5pm until 6:30pm
  • Wednesdays, from 5pm until 6:30pm
  • Thursdays, from 5pm until 6:30pm

Additional class opportunities

In addition to the regular classes, we also partner with the Apex Martial Arts and Yoga Tribe school to offer a weekly online cardio class as well as multiple weekly online Yoga Yin and Vinyasa classes. Scheduling and payment for these classes is handled through Apex.

  • Yin yoga online on Mondays, time TBD
  • Cardio class online on Tuesdays, 5pm until 5:30pm
  • Vinyasa yoga online on Wednesdays, time TBD
  • Yin yoga online on Thursdays, 6:45pm to 7:45pm

All online Apex classes are done over Zoom. Connect to Zoom using this link and wait for the owner of the class to let you into the call.

Cancellations

In the event that classes need to be cancelled, we will send an email out to the school email list notifying students of the cancellation. Time permitting we will also announce the cancellation on the school's Facebook page as well as on this website and in the school Calendar.
