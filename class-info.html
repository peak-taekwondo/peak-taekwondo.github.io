Class information

Classes are taught through RedLine Athletics as part of their RedLine Martial Arts program. All experience levels welcome! All classes are held at RedLine Athletics of Longmont at 700 9th Avenue.

Sign up for the classes you want to attend by going to their online class sign-up page.
